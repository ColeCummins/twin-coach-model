### **5\. Critical Risks & Transparency (Who is this for?)**

This is a specialized investment, and it is not for everyone. The tax benefits are subject to two key limitations:

1. **Tax Law Risk:** The 10.15% IRR is based on the **conservative,** current 40% bonus depreciation law **for 2025\.** If the deal is delayed to 2026, the rate drops to 20%, and the IRR falls. This creates a powerful "why now?" imperative.  
2. **Passive Activity Loss (PAL) Risk:** This is the most important qualifier. The IRS limits the use of "paper losses" for non-real-estate professionals. This investment is therefore ideal for two types of people:  
   * **"Qualified** Real Estate Professionals" (who can use the losses to offset their active income).  
   * **Individuals with significant existing "Passive Income"** from other rentals or businesses (who can use these losses to offset that income).
